Publication history

Preeiere

Following the success of the Fantastic Four in 1961 and the subsequent revival of American comic books featuring superheroes, Marvel Comics created new superhero characters. Stan Lee developed the initial concept for Iron Man

Solo series

Iron Man's solo comic book history began in 1968 after he transitioned from the anthology series Tales of Suspense into his own dedicated title. Over several decades, the character has headlined multiple volumes and major story arcs.

Fictional character biogrophy

Iron Man is the superhero alter-ego of Anthony "Tony" Stark, a billionaire industrialist and genius inventor co-created by Stan Lee, Larry Lieber, Don Heck, and Jack Kirby. Since his 1963 debut, the character has evolved from a Cold War-era weapons manufacturer into a pivotal figure in global defense and a founding member of the Avengers.
